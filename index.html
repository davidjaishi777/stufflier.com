STUFFLIER Pvt.Ltd. is an Jaishi Corporate established company by Mr.David Jaishi. Stufflier is made with two words "Stuff" and "Supplier".
This company works to transport and supply food, clothes, grocery and other stuffs from urban areas to the remote areas of Nepal by air and by road. It has been in force since 2033 A.D. It has been regulary supplying the items in the undeveloped parts of Nepal. The total turnover of this company has reached $50 Million.
